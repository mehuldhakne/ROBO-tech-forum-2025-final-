About Our Robotics Club

Welcome to the Robotech Club, where technology meets innovation! Our club is dedicated to exploring the exciting world of robotics, engineering, and automation.

Our Mission

To Our mission is to provide a platform for students to learn, experiment, and build robots that can solve real-world problems. We believe in fostering creativity and teamwork in the field of robotics.

What We Do

We work on various projects, from simple robots to complex autonomous systems. Our club members have access to state-of-the-art tools and resources to bring their ideas to life.. Our club offers comprehensive programs designed to provide practical experience with modern robotics technologies:

  • Robotics Workshops: Hands-on sessions covering programming, electronics, mechanical design, and system integration
  • Competition Teams: Participation in DD Robocon, IRoC-U, and regional engineering & Robotics competitions
  • Personal Projects: Collaborative work on autonomous systems, machine learning applications, and innovative prototypes
  • Industry Partnerships: Company Sponsorships and internship opportunities
  • Technical Skills Development: Training in CAD design, 3D printing, microcontroller programming, and project management

Our History

Founded with a vision to democratize robotics education, our club has grown from a small group of enthusiasts into a thriving community of innovators.

2018
Club founded by five engineering students with a shared passion for robotics and automation technology.
2019
First competition entry in regional VEX Robotics tournament, establishing our competitive program foundation.
2020
Expanded to 50+ members and launched our first community outreach program for middle school students.
2021
Won first major competition award and established partnerships with three local engineering firms.
2022
Opened dedicated robotics laboratory and launched advanced research project initiatives.
2023
Reached 100+ active members and began hosting annual regional robotics symposium.
2024
Established alumni mentorship network and launched scholarship program for underrepresented students in STEM.
